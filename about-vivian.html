About Vivian Zapanta

A Philippine Fitness Icon

Mentor, Trainer of Trainers, & Lifelong Student

A Philippine icon in the fitness industry, studio owner Vivian Zapanta is a Merrithew Lead Instructor Trainer — qualified to teach and train other STOTT PILATES® Instructor Trainers through numerous workshops.

Vivian loves life and lives it to the MAX… she is funny, witty and smart. She travels the world to teach, train and certify Pilates instructors as a Trainer of the Trainers. She is a mentor and mother not only to her daughter, Sheila, but also to her extended family of Fitness Trainers around the globe — most especially her team of Pilates Instructors trained here at her own studio.

Additional Credentials

A Never-Ending Student

Awarded Leadership & Mentoring Award by Merrithew Corporation, June 2012, Toronto, Canada.

GOLD ACE Certified Personal Trainer since 1990; ACE Group Fitness Certified since 1989.

1st and only Franklin Method Educator Level 3 in Asia (Levels 1, 2 & 3 — Skeletal, Muscular, Internal Organs & Fascia).

1st Fascianator Method Educator in the Philippines.

Les Mills Internationally Certified (Body Balance, Body Pump, Body Jam) & certified Zumba Instructor.

Former Personal Trainer of Her Excellency Madame Gloria Macapagal Arroyo, and former DLSU Graduate School faculty member.

A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Education, Major in Physical Education, Cum Laude, University of Santo Tomas 1975. Former University of Santo Tomas Professor in Physical Education, 1975. For credential verification, visit her IDEA Fit profile.
