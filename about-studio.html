About Our Studio

Welcome

Welcome to Our Studio

We are a virtual studio with a physical home studio based in Parañaque, fully equipped with complete STOTT PILATES® equipment. Vivian Zapanta trains students here to become certified STOTT PILATES® Instructors, in person and online.

"Our studio is the 1st and only STOTT PILATES® Licensed Training Center in the Philippines."

Recognition

Leadership & Mentoring Award

Our studio has been honored with the Leadership & Mentoring Award from the Merrithew Health & Fitness Community of Excellence Program, in recognition of our commitment to nurturing the next generation of Pilates instructors.

"Vivian unselfishly shares all her knowledge, lengthy years experience and insights to students that seek her help..."

Why Train Here

10 Reasons to Practice Pilates at Our Studio

1

Intelligent Exercise

Every movement is purposeful, mindful, and adapted to your body's needs and goals.

2

Core Strength and Stability

Build strong arms, legs, and a stable center that supports everything you do.

3

Improves Posture and Alignment

Better alignment means easier breathing and more pain-free movement throughout your day.

4

Increases Flexibility

Greater flexibility helps prevent falls and injury, keeping you mobile at any age.

5

Improves Muscular Balance and Strength

Pilates rebalances muscle groups so no single area is overworked or underused.

6

Increases Muscular Endurance

Consistent practice builds the stamina to move well for longer, with less fatigue.

7

Prevents Injury and Heightens Body Awareness

Learning how your body moves helps you avoid the habits that lead to injury.

8

Enhances Athletic Performance

Athletes use Pilates to isolate and target sport-specific muscles for better performance.

9

Relieves Stress

Controlled breathing is central to Pilates, helping the body and mind relax.

10

Relieves Back Pain

Strengthening the core is one of the most effective ways to relieve and prevent back pain.
