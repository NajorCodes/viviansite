STOTT PILATES® Instructor Course Overview

All instructor candidates must complete Functional Anatomy as a prerequisite before proceeding into Level 1 coursework.

Level 1 Courses

  • Intensive Mat Plus
  • Intensive Reformer
  • Intensive Cadillac
  • Intensive Stability Chair
  • Intensive Barrels

Level 2 Courses

  • Advanced Mat
  • Advanced Reformer
  • Advanced Cadillac
  • Advanced Stability Chair
  • Advanced Barrels
  • Injuries & Special Population
