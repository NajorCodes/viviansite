Pilates Instructor Courses

Learn. Grow. Teach. Become a Pilates instructor and deepen your practice with us.

Certification Pathway

STOTT PILATES® Instructor Course Overview

Prerequisite

All instructor candidates must complete Functional Anatomy before proceeding into Level 1 coursework.

Level 1 Courses

Intensive Mat Plus+

Equipment needed: Mat, Fitness Circle, Hand Weights, Flex-Band

Intensive Reformer+

Equipment needed: Reformer (with box)

Intensive Cadillac+

Equipment needed: Cadillac / Trapeze Table

Intensive Stability Chair+

Equipment needed: Stability Chair

Intensive Barrels+

Equipment needed: Ladder Barrel, Spine Corrector/Arc Barrel

Level 2 Courses

Advanced Mat+

Equipment needed: Mat, Fitness Circle, Hand Weights, Flex-Band, Stability Ball

Advanced Reformer+

Equipment needed: Reformer (with box, Jump Board optional)

Advanced Cadillac+

Equipment needed: Cadillac / Trapeze Table

Advanced Stability Chair+

Equipment needed: Stability Chair

Advanced Barrels+

Equipment needed: Ladder Barrel, Spine Corrector/Arc Barrel

Injuries & Special Population+

Equipment needed: Mat, Reformer, Cadillac, Stability Chair

Downloadable course schedules are typically available for the 1st Quarter (Level 1 and Advanced Level 2). Contact us for the current schedule.
